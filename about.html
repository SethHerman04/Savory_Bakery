About Us

Savory Bakery, the place for your tasty pastries! Kneading out smiles and delight since 2014. We are a local, family owned and operated bakery, specializing in many hand crafted breads, cookies, pies, and pastries that are sure to whisk you away!

We begun small, with nothing but a dream of creating a warm and welcoming atmosphere, while serving the best baked goods available. The journey has been a bit of a trial by fire, but much like bread in the oven, it has allowed us to rise up.

Step in and experience our warm, fresh baked goods, in a delightful atmosphere. A trip to Savory Bakery is sure to leave you feeling rye-ly good! We look forward to meeting you!
